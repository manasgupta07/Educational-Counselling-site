On the Postgraduate Diploma in Education - Counselling and Guidance from Massey University you’ll develop theoretical knowledge relevant to effective counselling and guidance practice and roles. This diploma is also the first step if you’re thinking of taking the advanced professional qualification, Master of Counselling Studies, and gaining professional membership to practise as a counsellor.
